Privacy Policy OmniTrain · Last updated: 4 July 2026 The short version: OmniTrain is a personal training log that runs entirely on your device. It does not have accounts, does not require an internet connection to work, and does not send your training data, nutrition data, measurements, or photos anywhere. Everything you enter stays on your phone. 1. Who this policy is for This policy explains how OmniTrain (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles your information. It applies to the OmniTrain app on iOS and Android. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app. 2. Information stored on your device OmniTrain stores the information you enter so it can show you your history and progress. This is kept locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or to anyone else. It includes: Workout and training data — exercises, sets, reps, weights, durations, rest timers, and session notes. Nutrition and hydration data — foods you log, portion amounts, and water intake. Body measurements and profile details you choose to enter, such as height and weight. An optional profile photo, and optional photos you attach to foods. Your app preferences and settings, such as theme and units. We do not have access to any of this. It is not backed up to a server we control, and we cannot see, retrieve, or recover it. 3. Information we collect None. OmniTrain does not collect personal information, does not create user accounts, contains no analytics or tracking, contains no advertising, and does not share or sell any information to third parties. Because your data never leaves your device, there is nothing for us to collect, store, or disclose. 4. Device permissions the app uses OmniTrain asks for a small number of system permissions only to provide specific features. You can grant or deny each one, and you can change your choice at any time in your device settings. Photos: so you can choose an image from your device to use as a profile picture or to attach to a food you log. The app only accesses the specific image you select. Notifications: so the app can alert you when a rest timer finishes, including when the app is in the background. Scheduling exact alarms (Android): so rest-timer alerts fire at the precise time they are due rather than being delayed by the system. These permissions are used solely for the features described above. The app does not use them to identify you or to gather information about you. 5. Third-party services OmniTrain does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social services that would receive your data. The app uses standard operating-system components provided by Apple and Google to display content and manage on-device storage; these are governed by Apple’s and Google’s own privacy policies. 6. Keeping and deleting your data Your data remains on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. You are in control of it: You can edit or delete individual entries within the app. Uninstalling OmniTrain removes the app’s data from your device. Because your data is stored only on your device, we are unable to delete it for you or restore it if it is lost — for example, if you uninstall the app or reset your device without keeping your own copy. 7. Children’s privacy OmniTrain does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Because the app collects no personal information and transmits no data, it does not gather information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your region). 8. Changes to this policy If the app changes in a way that affects how information is handled — for example, if a future version adds optional cloud sync or accounts — this policy will be updated and the “Last updated” date above will change. Material changes will be reflected here before the relevant feature is released. 9. Contact If you have any questions about this policy, contact: ezhix46@gmail.com.
